How it works?

  • You can add up to 10 tasks using the "Add" button.
  • To mark a task as completed, simply click on tick button. You can also undo this action if the task is not yet completed.
  • If you wish to delete a task, click on the delete icon beside the task.
  • Remember, the app supports a maximum of 10 tasks at a time.
